How To Make Sadza

Cooking Time:

30-55 mins

Ingredients You'll Need:

  • Corn Meal
  • Collard Greens or Mustard Greens
  • Meat(can be any protein you want)
  • Soup

Steps:

For Corn Meal->

  1. Mix 1 cup of corn meal with 3 cups of water
  2. Add into a large pot and mix into a paste, then let the mixture start to boil.
  3. Keep mixing the boiling liquid unti it is smooth, making sure to break up any lumps that form.
  4. Once the mixture begins to thicken, keep on low heat until it thickens more, then let it sit and cool down for it to harden more.

For Vegetables->

  1. Place cut up vegetables into heated pot on stove.
  2. Cook down the vegtables until they decrease in size and are more soft.
  3. You can now add more tomatoes or onions depedning on preference.
  4. Once the vegetables are softer, you can put them to the side.

For Meat/Soup->

  1. Cook the protein as you normally would.
  2. Once finished, add soup of your choice, I would recommend Royco, to the meat to give it more flavor.

Now, you're ready to enjoy your hearty meal of sadza you made!
